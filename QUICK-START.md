# Quick Start Guide

## Get Started in 3 Steps

### Step 1: Open the App
- Open `index.html` in your phone's browser (Safari on iPhone, Chrome on Android)

### Step 2: Add to Home Screen
**iPhone:**
- Tap Share button → "Add to Home Screen" → Add

**Android:**
- Tap menu (3 dots) → "Add to Home Screen" → Add

### Step 3: Start Tracking!
- Tap the app icon
- Select Office or Non-Office day
- Tap YouTube links to start workouts
- Log your weight weekly
- Check your progress!

## First Time Setup

1. **Set Start Date** (if needed)
   - The app automatically uses today as Day 1
   - To change, you can clear browser storage and reload

2. **First Workout**
   - Go to "Workout" tab
   - Select your day type (Office or Non-Office)
   - Tap the YouTube links
   - Check off items as you complete them

3. **First Weight Entry**
   - Go to "Weight" tab
   - Enter your starting weight (83.0 kg)
   - Tap "Add"
   - This becomes your baseline

## Daily Routine

**Morning:**
1. Open the app
2. Check today's workout
3. Complete the workout
4. Check off your checklist

**Weekly (Sunday evening):**
1. Weigh yourself
2. Enter weight in the app
3. Check your progress dashboard
4. Celebrate your wins!

## Tips

- ✅ The app saves everything automatically
- ✅ Works offline (except YouTube videos)
- ✅ No account needed - all data stays on your phone
- ✅ Your progress is private and secure
